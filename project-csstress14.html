Tech Stack

The Problem

For our CSE 110 team capstone, my four-person team needed to design and ship a working web application from scratch over a single quarter, applying agile practices — sprint planning, meeting notes, PR review — to a real product decision instead of a toy exercise. We landed on a task/backlog management tool, aimed at small teams who wanted something lighter than Jira.

My Role

I built out the Cloudflare Workers API backend: the task CRUD endpoints, including create, update, and the DELETE /api/tasks/:id route, plus fixes to keep the task description field syncing correctly between the frontend form and the worker on save. I also reviewed teammates' pull requests and contributed to our meeting notes and design brief.

Outcome

The project shipped as a fully working full-stack app with 272 commits across the team, a real collaboration history of design docs and PRs, and a recorded project demo. It's the most complete, most current project I can point to for full-stack development and API design.
