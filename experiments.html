Extra Credit: MathML

Here's Einstein's mass–energy equivalence formula, written with MathML instead of an image:

E = m c 2

MathML has technically been part of the HTML spec for a long time, but for years it wasn't something developers could actually rely on. According to caniuse.com, Chrome (and Chromium-based browsers like Edge) didn't ship built-in MathML support until relatively recently — for a long stretch, Chrome either had no support or required a polyfill like MathJax, even though Firefox and Safari had supported it for years. That's a big deal because Chrome/Chromium has such a large browser market share: a spec feature is only really usable in production once it works consistently across the browsers most of your users actually have.
