Tech Stack

TypeScriptAngularAES

The Problem

Most of my other projects are CRUD apps or frameworks practice — I wanted something small and focused that let me actually get hands-on with symmetric encryption instead of just reading about how AES works.

My Role

Solo project. I built a small Angular tool that takes plain text and a key, and encrypts or decrypts it using AES, with a simple form-driven interface for trying it out.

Outcome

A working encrypt/decrypt tool that's topically distinct from my CRUD-style apps — it pairs well with GriffinBlockchain to show a security-minded thread across my projects, rather than just framework repetition.
